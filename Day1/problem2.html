Meghana Foods

About Restaurent

Meghana Foods is a popular casual dining and delivery restaurant group in Bengaluru known especially for Andhra/Hyderabadi biryani, spicy rice dishes, and a mix of Indian (Andhra, South Indian, North Indian) and seafood options. It is often praised for its hearty portions and bold flavours.

Menu Categories

  • Non veg Biryani and rice
  • Veg Biryani and rice
  • Starters
  • Curries and Gravies dishes
  • Seafood
  • Breads and Extras
  • Beverages and Desserts
